This is Serial version NPB benchmark for testing our Paging implementation for Nautilus. 

Step1: specify your nautilus path under the src/NPB_SER/config/make.def

Step2: make the benchmark, etc. "make IS CLASS=S"

Step3: Turn on the NAUT_CONFIG_RUN_TESTS_AT_BOOT macro at /src/arch/x64/init.c

Step4: Compile and run Nautilus
